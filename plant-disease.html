Plant Disease Detection Solution

Helping farmers with early identification to reduce crop losses and improve yield

Our Plant Disease Detection system leverages advanced image processing and deep learning techniques to identify plant diseases from leaf images. This solution empowers farmers and agronomists with early detection capabilities, enabling preventive actions that significantly reduce crop losses and improve overall yield.

Key Features

  • Real-time Detection: Farmers can upload or capture leaf images and get instant results
  • High Accuracy Classification: Deep learning models (CNNs, Vision Transformers) ensure precise disease recognition
  • Multi-crop Support: Covers multiple plant species for broad agricultural applications
  • Early Disease Identification: Detects symptoms at an early stage for preventive action
  • Real-world Datasets: Trained on publicly available datasets like PlantVillage, plus field-collected images
