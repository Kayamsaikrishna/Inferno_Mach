AI Quality Control Solution

Automated material quality assessment according to ASTM A247 & ISO 945-1 standards

Our AI Quality Control system is a computer vision solution designed for automated analysis of cast iron microstructures according to ASTM A247 & ISO 945-1 standards. Utilizing industrial cameras for real-time material quality assessment, this solution revolutionizes quality control processes in manufacturing environments.

Key Features

  • Live Detection: Real-time microstructure analysis during production
  • Labor Reduction: Automates hours of manual microscopy work
  • Time Efficiency: Instant results vs. traditional multi-hour processes
  • Consistent Quality: Eliminates human error and subjective interpretation
  • Standards Compliance: Automatic verification against ASTM/ISO standards
